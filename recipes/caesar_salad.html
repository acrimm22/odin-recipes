Caesar Salad

Description

This simple, yet satisfying caesar salad hits just the right spot. A perfect side dish for any night of the week. Easily customizable and fresh ingredients!

Ingredients

For the Courtons
  • Half French Baguette, cut in thinly sliced strips about 0.25 inch thick.
  • 3 Tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 2 Tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated
Caesar Salad Dressing
  • 2 small garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1.5 teaspoons red wine vinegar
  • 0.33 cups extra virgin olive oil
  • 0.5 teaspoons sea salt, or to taste
  • 0.125 teaspoons black pepper
For the Caesar Salad
  • 1 large romaine lettuce
  • 0.33 cups parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved

Steps

How to Make Croutons
  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Cut the baguette in half lengthwise through the top of the baguette then slice diagonally into 0.25 inch thick pieces. Place the bread pieces onto a baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, combine 3 Tablespoons extra virgin olive oil and 1 teaspoon of finely minced garlic. Drizzle the garlic oil over the croutons and sprinkle the top with 2 Tablespoons grated parmesan cheese.
  3. Toss until evenly coated. Spread in a single layer over the baking sheet and bake at 350F until light golden and crisp, about 10 to 12 minutes, or to desired crispness.
How to Make Caesar Salad Dressing
  1. In a small bowl, whisk together garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice, and red wine vinegar.
  2. Slowly drizzle in extra virgin olive oil while whisking constantly.
  3. Whisk in 0.5 teaspoons salt and 0.125 teaspoons black pepper, or season to taste.
How to Make Caesar Salad
  1. Rinse, dry, and chop or tear the romaine into bite-sized pieces. Place in a large serving bowl and sprinkle generously with shredded parmesan cheese and cooled croutons. Drizzle with caesar dressing and toss gently until lettuce is evenly coated.
